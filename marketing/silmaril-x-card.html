Silmaril

The problems I kept having.

I did not want another standalone AI browser. I did not want another subscription. And I did not want to spin up Playwright or Puppeteer just to get through a messy real-life browser task in my actual logged-in browser.

What was not working

AI browsers want me to switch into another browser.
A lot of them want a separate paid product.
Playwright and Puppeteer are great, but they are built for heavier automation.
For small one-off tasks, writing and maintaining scripts is overkill.
Real browser work is often messy, interactive, and happens in my actual logged-in browser.

So I built Silmaril

Silmaril is a skill for Codex and Claude.
Built for everyday immediate browser situations.
It lets them run browser commands directly in the terminal.
It uses your real browser, not a separate AI browser or a full automation setup.

The goal was simple.

Let Codex or Claude run browser commands directly from the terminal in the browser I already use.
